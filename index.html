Data Deletion Request

In compliance with Facebook's data protection regulations, you have the right to request the deletion of your data collected by our app.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to request the deletion of your data, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to support@myapp.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  • Include your Facebook User ID or the email address associated with your Facebook account.
  • We will process your request within 30 days and confirm once your data has been permanently deleted from our systems.

If you have any questions, contact us at support@myapp.com.
